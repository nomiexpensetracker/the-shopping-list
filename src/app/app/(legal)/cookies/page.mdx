# Cookies

**Last updated:** April 16, 2026

This page explains what cookies and browser storage The Shopping List uses, why, and how you can control them.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser by a website. They persist across browser sessions and can be read back on future visits.

---

## 2. Cookies We Set

**The Shopping List's own servers set no cookies.** We do not use session cookies, authentication cookies, or any server-side tracking cookies.

The only cookies that may be present in your browser are those set by **Google Analytics 4**, and only if you have accepted our cookie consent.

### Google Analytics 4 Cookies

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `_ga` | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Distinguishes users within a 24-hour window | 24 hours |
| `_ga_EG3HB87D9P` | Stores session state for GA4 | 1 year |

**These cookies are only set after you explicitly click "Accept" on the cookie consent banner.** If you click "Decline", GA4 is never loaded and none of these cookies are set.

---

## 3. Browser Storage (Not Cookies)

We use your browser's `localStorage` for app functionality. Unlike cookies, `localStorage` data is **not sent to our servers automatically** — it stays on your device.

| Key | Contents | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `lists_registry` | List tokens | Tracks which shopping lists belong to this browser |
| `participant_<sessionId>_id` | Participant token | Identifies you within a specific session |
| `participant_<sessionId>_name` | Your nickname | Restores your name when you re-visit a session |
| `participant_<sessionId>_color` | Your avatar color | Restores your colour when you re-visit a session |
| `cookie_consent` | `"accepted"` or `"declined"` | Remembers your cookie consent choice |

---

## 4. Your Consent

When you first visit The Shopping List, a consent banner appears at the bottom of the screen. You have two choices:

- **Accept** — Google Analytics is enabled. GA4 cookies will be set in your browser.
- **Decline** — Google Analytics is never loaded. No GA4 cookies are set.

Your choice is stored in `localStorage` under the key `cookie_consent`. Once you make a choice, the banner will not appear again.

---

## 5. How to Manage or Withdraw Consent

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by:

1. **Clearing your browser's local storage** for this site — your choice will be reset and the consent banner will re-appear on your next visit.
2. **Using your browser's cookie settings** to delete the `_ga`, `_gid`, and `_ga_EG3HB87D9P` cookies directly.
3. **Installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)** — this prevents GA4 from collecting data across all websites.

Instructions for managing cookies and local storage:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)

---

## 6. Third-Party Analytics (No Cookies)

**Vercel Analytics** and **Vercel Speed Insights** are also active on this site. These tools collect anonymous, aggregated performance and usage data and do **not** set any persistent cookies. They are not subject to cookie consent.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

If we change the cookies we use, we will update this page and the effective date. We will re-prompt for consent if the nature of cookie use changes materially.
